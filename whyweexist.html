Why We Exist

  • Creating Jobs: By expanding cassava production and export operations, we generate both direct employment in farming and processing, and indirect jobs across logistics, marketing, and supply chains.
  • Raising Household Incomes: We empower rural farmers and producers by providing stable market access, fair pricing, and capacity building, leading to increased and reliable income for households.
  • Uplifting Ugandan Communities: Our work strengthens local economies, supports entrepreneurship, and reinvests in community development through inclusive partnerships and training programs.
  • Promoting Uganda on the Global Stage: Through high-quality cassava exports and other ventures, we showcase the richness and reliability of Ugandan agriculture and talent to international markets.

We believe in sustainable growth that benefits everyone from the farmer to the final consumer creating value at every step of the journey.

HERAD Intercontinental Services Ltd

We empower farmers and connect Uganda to the world through the export of high-quality cassava. Our commitment is to agricultural transformation, job creation, and sustainable value chains.
