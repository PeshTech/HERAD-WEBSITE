Our Impact

Since our establishment, HERAD has played a key role in Uganda’s economic growth by focusing on high-impact areas that touch lives and uplift communities:

Job Creation

We have generated both direct and indirect employment opportunities, improving livelihoods across local communities.

Export Growth

By connecting Ugandan farmers to international markets, we’ve strengthened the agricultural sector and boosted national export revenues.

SME Support

Through business development and hands-on training programs, we empower small entrepreneurs to scale and thrive.

Tourism Promotion

We promote Uganda’s natural and cultural heritage globally, contributing to a more vibrant and resilient tourism industry.
